About Aikhon Technologies

Aikhon Technologies engineers next-generation PLCs and automation systems for industrial, agricultural, vehicular, and robotic applications. Our goal is to deliver compact, intelligent controllers that combine real-time reliability with modern connectivity and update capabilities.

We design hardware for harsh environments, where uptime matters and systems must perform consistently — from farm machinery and trucks to robotics and fixed infrastructure. Our products bridge the gap between traditional PLCs and modern IoT-enabled controllers.

  • Modular designs that scale with your application

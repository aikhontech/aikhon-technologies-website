About Aikhon Technologies

Aikhon Technologies is focused on practical, reliable control systems for real equipment: trucks, machinery, robotics, and connected devices.

Our goal is to bridge the gap between off-the-shelf PLCs and custom electronics by providing robust hardware with user-friendly software tools.
